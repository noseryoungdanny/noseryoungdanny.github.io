Yeast dumplings filled
with Nutella (Buchteln)


Download recipe

Step 1

Let's go: To begin, heat 250 ml of milk with 75 g of butter in a small saucepan until the butter is completely melted.

It should look like this:

Step 2

Next, mix 500 g of wheat flour with 1 packet of dry yeast in a mixing bowl, followed by 50 g of sugar, 1 packet of vanilla sugar, 1 egg (size M), 1 pinch of salt and 4 drops of lemon flavoring.

Step 3

Then process all the ingredients with the dough hook of the mixer on high speed for about 5 minutes to a firm dough. Then cover the dough with a tea towel and place it in a warm place. Let the dough rise there for about 20 minutes until it has visually increased.

It should look like this:
It should look like this:

Step 4

Next, mix 500 g of wheat flour with 1 packet of dry yeast in a mixing bowl, followed by 50 g of sugar, 1 packet of vanilla sugar, 1 egg (size M), 1 pinch of salt and 4 drops of lemon flavoring.

Step 5

When the dough has visibly increased in size, take it out of the mixing bowl and knead it briefly on a lightly floured work surface. The flour will prevent the dough from sticking to your work surface. Then roll the dough into a roll and divide it into 13 equal pieces. This is best done with a dough card.

It should look like this:

Step 6

Then roll the individual pieces of dough into balls.

Step 7

Next, roll out each dough ball briefly with a rolling pin. Then you need 13 level tablespoons of Nutella. Then spread 1 tablespoon of the Nutella on each individual dough ball.

It should look like this:
It should look like this:

Step 8

Now press the dough together around the Nutella filling and place the balls, opening side down, into the prepared baking dish. Then put them evenly into the baking dish. Before you put them in, you should grease the baking dish with butter. Now, Let them rest for 30 minutes.

Step 8

Now press the dough together around the Nutella filling and place the balls, opening side down, into the prepared baking dish. Then put them evenly into the baking dish. Before you put them in, you should grease the baking dish with butter. Now, Let them rest for 30 minutes.

Step 9

Before you put them in the oven, melt 20 g of butter in a small saucepan. Then grab a pastry brush and brush your Buchteln with the melted butter to make them nice and moist.

Step 10

Then put them in the oven for 20 minutes. They will be best if you place the baking dish in the lower third of the oven. After the baking time, take them out of the oven and let the baking dish cool on a rack for 10 min.

Step 11

Enjoy!
